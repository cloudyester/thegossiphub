Skincare

How to look after your skin daily.

The Gossip Hub

Cleansing Routine

Wash your face every morning and night.

Use a gentle cleanser suitable for your skin type to remove dirt and oils.

Hydration Basics

Moisturise daily to avoid dryness.

Moisturiser helps protect your skin barrier and keeps your skin soft and healthy.

Sunscreen Daily

Protect your skin from UV rays.

Apply sunscreen even when indoors to reduce damage and maintain even tone.

Gentle Exfoliation

Remove dead skin cells.

Exfoliating 1–2 times per week helps smooth texture and improve glow.

Face Masks

Give your skin an extra boost.

Clay masks, sheet masks, or gel masks support hydration and clarity.

Know Your Skin Type

Different routines fit different skin.

Identify whether your skin is oily, dry, combination, or normal for best results.

Patch Testing

Check new products safely.

Always test new skincare on a small area before applying it to your whole face.

Night Routine

Repair your skin while you sleep.

Night creams and serums work best when your skin is resting.

Avoid Overwashing

Too much cleansing harms your skin.

Washing too often strips oils and can cause irritation.

Hands Off

Touching your face spreads germs.

Avoid resting your hands on your face to prevent irritation.

Clean Makeup Tools

Brushes can hold dirt and oils.

Wash them weekly to maintain good skin hygiene.

Stay Hydrated

Water is important for skin.

Drinking enough water helps maintain skin elasticity and glow.

Healthy Diet

Food affects your skin health.

Fruits, vegetables, and balanced meals support clearer skin.

Avoid Harsh Scrubs

Some products damage skin.

Choose gentle products to avoid micro-tears and irritation.

Limit Fragrance

Fragrance can irritate sensitive skin.

Unscented products reduce the chance of discomfort.

Consistent Routine

Results come with time.

Sticking to your routine daily leads to healthier skin.
